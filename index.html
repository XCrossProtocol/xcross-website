Mission

XCross is a Web3-native loyalty and incentive protocol that empowers DeFi projects and ecosystems to launch outcome-driven, multi-tiered points programs. Inspired by airline mileage systems and evolved for the blockchain era, XCross enables protocols to reward user adoption, liquidity contribution, and social engagement through the XPT token. Whether isolated or collaborative, these programs help drive user acquisition, retention, and ecosystem-wide synergy—creating a unified standard for incentivizing behavior across the decentralized economy.

Team

The XCross team is a collective of seasoned Web3 builders, product strategists, and DeFi veterans with deep experience across protocol design, tokenomics, and user growth. With roots in leading blockchain ventures and a track record of scaling ecosystems, the team brings a mission-driven mindset to redefining loyalty and engagement in decentralized finance. We’re passionate about creating infrastructure that drives real adoption, cross-protocol collaboration, and long-term value for users and partners alike.
